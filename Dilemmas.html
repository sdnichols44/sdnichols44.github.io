Dilemmas Compiler

Build your Dilemma here! Unlike most other compilers, there are multiple compilers on this page, one for each piece of the Dilemma.

Dilemmas is more complicated than most other assets, therefore if you are not sure how to build and implement a Dilemma, then please consult the info page. You will need to build all 4 compulsory parts separately, and transfer each to its own line in Dilemmas.txt. Some fields require you to reference later or previous parts of the dilemma so that the game can link them together and display them properly in-game. If you do not create the links correctly, then you may experience anything from your dilemma not appearing in-game, the dilemma showing but is bugged in some fashion, or even game crashes. It is therefore essential you know where your references need to be and what they should be pointing to.
