Media Reports Compiler

Build your Media Report here! Unlike most other compilers, there are multiple compilers on this page, one for each piece of the Report. When you compile the header, the fields that are required to be identical for all parts of the report body are automatically filled for you, to prevent mismatches. However, the fields are left available to you if for some reason you wish to change them to something else (but then your report won't function correctly.

Unlike most other sections, but similar to Dilemmas, MediaReports.txt has multiple separate sections that all fit together like a puzzle. The header will set a reference that is then used for the first part of the body to match them together, which is a process repeated for any further body sections. Header leads to Body0, Body0 to Body1 and so on.

Your Media Report must have a header, and can have a number of body sections, though usually a maximum of 5. As with Dilemmas, if your sections are not properly linked, you will encounter a range of issues that can be from your report never spawning, blank or error filled reports, all the way up to and including crashing the game.
