Personality Trait Compiler

Build your personality trait here. Enter your desired values into the inputs below, then click on 'Compile Trait!' at the bottom to have the string automatically compiled for you.

Basic Trait Details - Set the unique Trait ID, Trait Name and Trait Flavour Text.

Requirements and Settings - Set the conditions where a Trait can occur, how long it lasts, and it's interaction with other Traits.

Emails and Messages - Set additional messages that are sent to the player when your trait is rolled or expires on all drivers, just your own, or none at all.

Base Driver Stat Modifiers - Set the effect of your trait on the driver's core stats, either all at once with the use of Overall, or just on specific stats.

Special Cases - Set whether your trait will only work in specific circumstances, for example during a race, or if the driver is in a specific series. Check Info for the full list of Special Cases.

Other Stat Modifiers - Set the effect your trait will have on non-driving stats such as Marketability and Chairman Happiness.
